## Detailed Description
Off-Grid Scout analyses UK property listings in real-time, giving you an instant Sovereignty Score that rates any plot's potential for off-grid, self-sufficient living.

🏕️ WHAT IT DOES
Browse Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket, Savills, PrimeLocation, or PlotFinder as normal. When you find a property, click the Off-Grid Scout icon to instantly scan it against 7+ real data sources:

⚡ Energy — Solar irradiance data from NASA POWER
💧 Water — Borehole viability and rainfall data from BGS
🌊 Flood Risk — Environment Agency flood zone mapping
🏗️ Planning — Ghost planning application detection via PlanIt
📶 Grid Distance — How far you really are from civilisation
☀️ Solar Potential — Annual solar generation estimates

📊 HOW SCORING WORKS
Each property gets a Sovereignty Score from 0-100 based on weighted analysis of all factors. Higher scores mean better off-grid potential.

• 70-100: Excellent — Strong candidate for self-sufficient living
• 40-69: Moderate — Possible with some compromises
• 0-39: Challenging — Significant obstacles to off-grid setup
