Frequently Asked Questions

What property sites does it work with?
Off-Grid Scout works with any UK property listing website including Rightmove, OnTheMarket, Zoopla, LandForSale.co.uk, and more. Simply browse a listing and click the extension icon.
What is a "Sovereignty Score"?
The Sovereignty Score (0-100) is our proprietary rating that combines planning policy analysis (40%), energy potential (30%), and water/resource availability (30%). Higher scores mean better off-grid potential. Scores above 70 are considered excellent candidates.
What is a "Ghost Application"?
A Ghost Application is a previously refused residential planning application near your target plot. Under Paragraph 84 of the NPPF, these refused applications can actually strengthen your case for alternative rural development. Our AI detects these automatically — a feature no other tool offers.
How accurate is the data?
We pull from official UK government and scientific sources: Environment Agency (flood), NASA POWER (energy), British Geological Survey (water/soil), OpenStreetMap (infrastructure), and PlanIt (planning history). The data is as accurate as the source agencies provide. We always recommend professional surveys before purchase.
Does it work outside the UK?
Currently Off-Grid Scout is UK-only, as it relies on UK-specific data sources (Environment Agency, BGS, UK planning system). We're exploring expansion to other countries in the future.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, cancel anytime with one click from your dashboard. No lock-in contracts, no cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
